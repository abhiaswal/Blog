Paragliding In Dehradun

We are awaiting a return to the outdoor culture in travel, and paragliding in Dehradun is one way to get started. From the glistering reflection of the Shivalik range to the stunning views of Doon Valley to the majestic peaks of Top Tibba, and a never seen perspective of Surkanda Devi, Dehradun has long lured adventurers to travel across the region. Experiencing these places is exclusive to an adventure sport that most of us can find scribbled on our bucket list—paragliding. Dehradun is home to countless cliffs and forestland areas that are home to paragliding bases, locations that offer a bird’s eye view of some popular landmarks and valleys. In this practical guide, we talk about flying over this popular tourist destination with a comprehensive guide including all you need to know before you plan your trip.
